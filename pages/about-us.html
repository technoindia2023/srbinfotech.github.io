The Directors

Suresh Dangi, an MBA Post Graduate by qualification, brings to the firm an industry experience of about seventeen years, of which a decade has been in the Linux domain, Suresh, also a Redhat Certified Linux Professional, Red Hat Certified Engineer.

Bharat Lalwani has more than a few internationally renowned certification to his credit – Red Hat Certified Engineer, Cisco Certified Network Associate and Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer. As the CTO of the Organization, Bharat is mentor and guide to the technical team.

Rajesh Shukla , an entrepreneur throughout, has been associated with IT since 2006. Having experience in various domains like Desktop Publishing and CAD, he has been associated with Linux and training for the last six years. Rajesh, also a Redhat Certified Linux Professional, Red Hat Certified Engineer and a Certified VMWare Specialist, heads the Business Division at Infraneed Infotech.

The Team

The team of engineers, headed by a Technical Manager, is chosen and groomed with a great deal of care and effort. Ranging from the freshers to the experienced, each member of the team is thoroughly trained on the skills needed to ensure a job is done professionally and efficiently. Every member of the team is required to reinforce and enhance his technical skills at periodic intervals, in order to stay abreast of the latest in the technologies that the organization has business interests in.
