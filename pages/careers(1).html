||Careers at Infraneed

As an organization, Infraneed Infotech provides ample opportunity for growth for the right candidate, with the right attitude and skills. Willingness to work hard and ability to learn fast is a must. We provide our employees with not only the latest in technical skills, but also an environment that invigorates and encourages the pursuit of excellence in the chosen profession.

Every member of the team at Infraneed is chosen and groomed with a great deal of care and effort. Ranging from the experienced to the freshers, each member is thoroughly trained on the skills needed to ensure a task is executed professionally and efficiently. Every member of the team is required to reinforce and enhance his / her skills at periodic intervals, in order to stay abreast of the latest in technology, and with the various offerings of our principals.

While age, sex, formal education and experience is no bar for any of the profiles, the applicant must be confident, reasonably fluent in English and must also possess good interpersonal skills. We are also looking for strong problem solving capabilities. The candidate may be required to travel to various client sites in India and abroad. Those interested may send us their resumes (mentioning relevant codes) by email at:jobs@infraneed.com.

__________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Infraneed Infotech  is looking for able candidates for the following roles

Linux SysAdmins

We are looking to recruit System Administrators at various levels. Those intending to apply must possess some Linux certification and reasonable skills on Red Hat / Suse / Ubuntu Linux. The job involves working on rolling out and supporting commercial technologies (Linux, in general) as well as high-end technologies like Virtualization, Cloud Computing, Disaster Recovery, and High Availability Clustering.
Code : Linux

Business Development Executives

The candidate, preferably female, would be responsible for generating sales for various solutions and the stack of products, services and applications. We are looking for dynamic, outgoing candidates with a flair for technology and with an ability to correctly understand and interpret prospective clients’ IT needs. The candidate must be able to communicate well in English (spoken, as well as written) and must also possess basic computing skills.
Job Code : BDE

Web Designers

Positions are open for Web Designers having sound skills in writing and debugging PHP and HTML code. Those intending to apply must possess good skills on Photoshop, Flash & CorelDraw.
Job Code : Web

Database Administrators

We are looking to recruit Database Administrators at various levels. Those intending to apply must possess reasonable skills on Oracle / MySQL / PostgreSQL, and preferably some database certification. The candidate should be dynamic and outgoing, with a flair for technical knowledge and with an ability to correctly understand and interpret clients’ technical requirements. The candidate must be able to communicate well in English (spoken, as well as written) .
Job Code : DBA
